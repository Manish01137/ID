Women's T20 World Cup Overview

The ICC Women's T20 World Cup is the flagship international T20 tournament in women's cricket, featuring 10 qualifying nations. The format is a two-group league stage (five teams each), followed by a semi-final and final. India, Australia, England, South Africa, and New Zealand dominate the betting markets, but recent tournaments have seen upsets from West Indies and Pakistan.
