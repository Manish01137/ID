Why Kohli is a Betting Signal

Kohli's betting relevance comes from his consistency — he's one of the lowest standard-deviation batters in modern cricket. His "top batter" markets are nearly always priced lower than his historical top-score rate, because casual bookmakers over-correct for his age and inactivity gaps between series.
