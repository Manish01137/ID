PSL Overview

The Pakistan Super League is a six-team T20 franchise competition run by the PCB across Karachi, Lahore, Rawalpindi and Multan. PSL pitches are typically high-scoring, the death-overs can go for 60+ runs, and the league is known for its wild in-match swings — making it one of the most profitable tournaments for live in-play bettors.
