What Causes Delays?

Being honest about the ~2% of withdrawals that take longer:

  • Bank maintenance windows — some banks pause IMPS at night. Typical delay: 20–40 min.
  • UPI handle issues — if your UPI ID is inactive or wrongly typed, withdrawal returns to wallet within 30 min (no loss of funds).
  • Large amounts (₹1 lakh+) — trigger a 2FA check with our compliance team. Adds 5–10 min.
  • First-ever withdrawal — a brief verification to ensure withdrawal method matches deposit source. Adds 3–5 min.
  • Weekend bank hours — NEFT transfers slow over weekends. IMPS/UPI are unaffected.
